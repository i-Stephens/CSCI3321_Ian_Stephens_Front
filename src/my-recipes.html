Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Using a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat, pour the batter (1/4 cup of batter per pancake)
  4. Brown on both sides and serve
