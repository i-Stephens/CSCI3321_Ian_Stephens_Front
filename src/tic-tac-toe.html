Rules:

  • 2 players - Player X vs Player O
  • Players take turns placing marks (X/O) in the empty spaces of a 3x3 grid
  • The 1st player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins
  • Once all spaces are filled and the win condition is not met, the result is a tie
